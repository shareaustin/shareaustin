About ShareAustin

ShareAustin is an application with the intent to bring people together while getting more value out of our neglected belongings. Why not pull that old kayak out of the garage to share with a neighbor? Austin is a city well-known for its recreational ventures, and ShareAustin aims to make these fun, outdoor ventures readily available to those who don't have the means or storage to house every single tool needed to go camping, kayaking, or even rock climbing.

Get started today by creating your own ShareAustin profile! Once you have logged in, you will be able to rent items posted by other users, or post your own items for others to rent. Don't forget to rate your transactions and leave friendly feedback for items you've rented or for sellers whom you have rented from.
